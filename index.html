Introducing Encipher

The Ultimate Secure Communication Platform

Existing messaging apps rely on centralized servers, making them easy targets for cyberattacks. Encipher eliminates these risks by providing a decentralized, end-to-end encrypted communication platform that gives users full control over their data.

With personal server hosting, stronger encryption than WhatsApp & Signal, and offline capabilities, Encipher ensures that your messages, calls, and metadata remain 100% private.

  • No Third-Party Servers – Your data stays with you.
  • End-to-End Encryption – Unbreakable security for texts & calls.
  • Self-Destruct & Remote Wipe – Erase sensitive data anytime.
  • Works Even on 2G – Reliable in low-bandwidth conditions.
  • Offline Communication – Secure messaging without the internet.
Why Encipher Stands Out

Unmatched Security & Control

Unlike traditional messaging apps, Encipher is designed for ultimate privacy and data sovereignty. It offers cutting-edge security features that give users complete control over their communication.

Personal Server Hosting

Your Data, Your Rules – Encipher is hosted on your personal server, eliminating reliance on third-party infrastructure. No middlemen, no tracking, no breaches.
